My Favorite Dog Breed:


Golder Retrievers - They are my favorite dog out of them all because they are gentle yet playful. They are gentle giants, and even though they trigger my allergies I would love to own one!
Dobermans - They are much sweeter than everyone portrays them to be! They are loving and very protective, they are energetic yet calm. In the future I would love to rescue one from a shelter:)
Chihuahuas - They are funny and smart. They are also the perfect dogs for low mantenance as they do not have a history of health problems, they also have a very long lifespan averaging to 13-16 years!
